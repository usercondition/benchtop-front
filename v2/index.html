02

FAQ

The short version of how a job works. Anything not covered — just ask.

Do I need my own files?

No. Send an STL / fileset if you have one, or describe what you’re after — I can design it in CAD from sketches, photos, or measurements, or help you find an existing model.

What kinds of jobs do you take?

Anything that suits high-detail resin: miniatures and display pieces, prototypes, replacement and functional parts, and small production runs. If you’re not sure it fits, ask.

How does pricing work?

Every job is quoted individually based on size, quantity, complexity, and any design work involved. You’ll get a clear price before anything goes on the plate.

What condition do parts arrive in?

Cleaned, cured, and checked — ready for priming, painting, or use. Supports are removed unless we agree otherwise for fragile pieces.

How do I start?

Reach out with a file, photos, a link, or a short description. I’ll confirm feasibility, price, and timing — then the job goes on the bench.
