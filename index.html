FAQ

How a job works. Ask if something’s missing.

Do I need my own files?

No. Send an STL if you have one, or describe the part — CAD from sketches, photos, or measurements is available.

What jobs do you take?

High-detail resin work: miniatures, display pieces, prototypes, functional parts, and small batches. Unsure? Ask.

How does pricing work?

Quoted per job — size, quantity, complexity, and any design work. Clear price before anything hits the plate.

What condition do parts arrive in?

Cleaned, cured, checked. Ready for priming, painting, or use. Supports removed unless agreed otherwise.

How do I start?

Send a file, photos, a link, or a short brief. Feasibility, price, and timing confirmed first.
